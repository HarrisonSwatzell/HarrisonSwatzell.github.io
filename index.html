Beyond my professional endeavors, I am also a history major because I feel it is a subject that everyone should be taught if we want to build a better future. My studies have not only enhanced my skills for communication, critical thinking, and research but have also given me more of an understanding and appreciation for all the lessons that history is able to provide us with today.
